Analysis on Congressional Tweets Dataset

In this data analytics project, we aim to explore and analyze a dataset of congressional tweets from 2008-2017. We will investigate how the sentiment of these tweets has changed over time, and identify any trends or patterns that may exist. Additionally, we will analyze the most mentioned individuals or groups in congressional tweets and track changes over time to gain insights into political discourse on social media. Through this analysis, we hope to gain a deeper understanding of how social media is used by politicians and its impact on public opinion.
